What is AI-RationMitra?

AI-RationMitra is India's flagship digital transformation initiative for the Public Distribution System (PDS). Blending Artificial Intelligence, real-time stock monitoring, and voice-enabled chatbots, it simplifies how rural citizens access their entitled ration — empowering families, eliminating corruption, and bringing transparency at scale.

Key Services & Features

Voice-Driven Chatbot

Users can interact using just voice to know ration eligibility, available stock, or raise requests.

Real-Time Stock Insights

Ensures transparency in commodity availability — visible to users and admins in real time.

Quota & Eligibility Checker

AI-guided eligibility and monthly quota checking with localized suggestions.

Admin Control Panel

Admins can monitor usage, update commodities, and view community feedback with precision.

Impact on Society

Since its inception, AI-RationMitra has revolutionized rural rationing across India. From curbing leakages and eliminating middlemen to enabling doorstep clarity on government schemes, the system has already touched thousands of lives and is on a mission to touch millions. By making tech accessible, inclusive, and voice-first — we’ve turned ration into a right, not a privilege.
